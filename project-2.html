Project Overview

Fiber Fitness is your ultimate fitness companion, thoughtfully designed to make health and wellness more accessible and enjoyable. The app includes a comprehensive calorie tracker to help you monitor and manage your daily intake, ensuring you stay on track with your dietary goals. It offers personalized workout suggestions tailored to your fitness level, alongside video-based exercise demonstrations for proper form and technique.

A seamless QR code system simplifies attendance tracking, making it easy to log your workouts and stay consistent. Additionally, Fiber Fitness provides tailored food suggestions that align with your health objectives, helping you build a sustainable and balanced lifestyle.

Whether you’re aiming to lose weight, gain strength, or maintain your fitness, Fiber Fitness empowers you to achieve your goals efficiently while keeping all your progress organized in one place. It's more than an app—it’s your partner in achieving a healthier, happier you.
